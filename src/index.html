FAQ

Do I need to be at home while cleaning?

Many clients stay at home when they order cleaning for the first time. But the next time they prefer to go on business, leaving the keys to the cleaner or ordering the delivery of keys to work as well as to another convenient place.

Does the price depend on the size of the apartment?

From April 23, 2019, the basic cost of cleaning depends not only on the number of rooms and bathrooms, but also on the area of ​​the apartment. The larger the area of ​​the apartment, the more time the cleaner needs to clean, so the price for cleaning apartments in Minsk with an increased area becomes higher. If the area of ​​the apartment exceeds the standard, then a coefficient of 1.2 Standard sizes of apartments: — 1-room. apartment up to 40 m² — 2-room. apartment up to 60 m² — 3-room. apartment up to 75 m² - 4-room. apartment up to 95 m² - 5-room. apartment up to 110 m² We always meet our customers and, of course, we will not measure the area of ​​​​the apartment and argue over a few extra meters, but please take into account the degree of pollution and treat these circumstances with understanding.

How many bathrooms are considered if the bathroom and toilet are separate?

In the cost of cleaning the apartment, we take into account the bathroom, as an object consisting of a bath / shower and toilet. Therefore, if you have a separate bathroom - a bathroom and a separate toilet, then when placing an order, you need to specify one bathroom.

What if something disappears or the cleaner breaks?

The most important thing is not to worry. We are confident in our performers, but just in case, we keep our own insurance reserve, which is formed from deductions from the cost of each cleaning. We refused on principle to cooperate with insurance companies, most of which offer an unconditional deductible of $ 500, and limited indemnity, which in fact means coverage of damage at the expense of our company and an unpleasant delay in meager compensation for the client. We will reimburse you for the damage. In parallel, we will work with the cleaner - we have all the necessary data in order to establish the truth.

Can furniture be rearranged to clean?

Unfortunately no. Rearranging furniture and large items requires special skills and equipment, and since we have a predominantly female team, we do not perform such work.

What is included in the cost of window cleaning?

The cost of window cleaning includes washing glass from both sides, cleaning with brushes and washing the frame, inter-frame space, slopes and window sills. Therefore, the cost of window cleaning is indicated for one sash (deaf or opening). The price of window cleaning depends on the complexity of the pollution and the amount of work, the larger the window area and the more partitions, the more time the cleaner needs to clean the window. - sash up to 1.5 m high and up to 1 m wide = 5 rubles - enlarged sash from 1.5 m high and more than 1 m wide = 7.5 rubles - panoramic window up to 2.7 m high, up to 70 cm wide (opening to full height) = 7.5 rubles - a panoramic window up to 2.7 m high, up to 70 cm wide (the lower part is deaf) = 10 rubles - a blind window with a barrier (grille) on the inside = 5 rubles - roof windows = from 10 rubles - installation and dismantling of the barrier (grilles) = 10 rubles - removal of a protective film or adhesive tape = from 2 rubles per m / n - washing and cleaning mosquito nets = 5 rubles - washing and cleaning horizontal blinds = 5 rubles - window vents = 2.5 rubles - washing complex windows, heavily polluted, incl. after repair is calculated with a coefficient of 1.5 * prices are indicated for one unit * in wooden windows of the "Soviet model" each opening sash is considered * in the cold season a multiplying coefficient can be applied

I have a pet at home, how does this affect the cost?

Just like our customers, we love our pets and want their habitat to be clean and tidy. But everyone knows perfectly well what difficulties wool from our cats and dogs or garbage from parrots can deliver. For the cleaner, this means more time to change the water more frequently and wash the wool off the work equipment. Therefore, in the presence of pets, a multiplying factor of 1.2

Do your cleaners take out the trash?

Cleaning and garbage removal is included in the price of cleaning the apartment at the rate of: - Maintenance cleaning - one bag of 60 liters - General cleaning - two bags of 60 liters each. In this case, the removal is considered to be the movement of garbage from the customer's apartment to the garbage container in the yard of the customer's house.

I have a studio / kitchen combined with a room, what should I specify when ordering?

Cleaning the kitchen is different from cleaning the room, these are two different sets of work, even if there is no wall between the rooms. Therefore, when placing an order, you must specify a separate room and a separate kitchen.

Do I need to issue detergents or equipment to the cleaner?

The custom-made cleaner comes with everything you need - a bucket, a mop, napkins, rags for cleaning surfaces, sponges with an abrasive side, as well as garbage bags. Cleaning products - for the bathroom, glass, floor and universal - we also bring with us. Unlike ordinary household chemicals, professional products do not contain phosphates and toxins. They do not cause allergies, completely decompose in water and do not harm the environment.

Do you clean the inside of kitchen cabinets and appliances?

During standard cleaning, we wipe kitchen cabinets and all appliances from the outside. I also wash inside kitchen cabinets, kitchen appliances - oven, microwave, refrigerator, etc. These are additional options that you can specify when placing an order.
